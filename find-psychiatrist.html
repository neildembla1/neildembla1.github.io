Connect with psychiatrists of South Asian origin in South Carolina

Looking for a Psychiatrist?

SCIAPA is working to build a directory of South Asian psychiatrists practicing in South Carolina. This resource will help community members find culturally competent mental health care providers who understand the unique needs and cultural context of the South Asian community.

For Patients

If you're looking for a psychiatrist, please contact us through our contact form and we'll help connect you with appropriate resources.

For Psychiatrists

Are you a psychiatrist of South Asian origin practicing in South Carolina? We'd love to include you in our directory. Please contact us to be added to our resource list.
