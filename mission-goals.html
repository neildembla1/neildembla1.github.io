MISSION AND GOALS

Updated: April 2024

  1. To form an alliance between the psychiatrists of Indian origin in South Carolina.
  2. To provide guidance and support to psychiatrists of Indian origin in South Carolina residency programs as well as those early in their career.
  3. To promote interest in high schoolers as well as medical students of Indian origin to pursue a career in Psychiatry.
  4. To serve as a mental health resource for the Indian community in South Carolina including teenagers.
  5. To facilitate awareness of mental health issues within the Indian community by organizing in person as well as virtual events.
  6. To collaborate with other mental health organizations in the community like Mental Health of America and NAMI.
