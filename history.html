HISTORY

Updated: April 2024

The Indo American Psychiatric association - IAPA was founded in 1979 in NYC by 6 psychiatrists of Indian origin. The first president was Dr. Yosuf Haveliwala and the current president is Dr. Tarak Vasavada. More details at www.myiapa.org

The South Carolina chapter of the Indo American Psychiatric Association was founded by Dr. Geetika Dembla and established on the 16th of September 2023. After visiting the IAPA banquet at the 2023 APA meeting in San Francisco, Dr Dembla decided to create this chapter to bring together psychiatrists of Indian origin in South Carolina with the paramount goal of serving as a unified force to meet the mental health needs of the Indian community.
